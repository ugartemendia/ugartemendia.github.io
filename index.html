PhD page

This page is a quick reference to my PhD research and dissertation around FLOSS projects in the Spanish public administration.

Details

The research was carried out in 2014-15 and represents a comprehensive analysis on the different steps of traditional public policies analysis, based on a set of cases that took place within the 2003-2013 timeframe

The research dissertation can be found here. The research dissertation is publisehd with a Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 License

The dissertation was succesfully defended at the Deusto Business School on January 11th, 2016.
